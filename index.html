Honda Civic 2000 Subaru Imprenza 2007 Mitsubishi Lancer Evo VIII 2005

Honda Civic 2000

part of the sixth generation, is a lightweight and highly tunable car, popular among enthusiasts for its affordability and reliability.

  • Engine Options: D-series (1.6L SOHC) or B-series (1.6L DOHC VTEC).
  • Price Range (used): Affordable, around $3,000–$7,000 depending on condition.
  • Maintenance: Low cost; parts are widely available.

    • Subaru Impreza 2007

    part of the second generation (GD/GG), is renowned for its all-wheel-drive system and rally heritage.

  • Engine Options: 2.5L Turbocharged Flat-4 (WRX/STI models).
  • Price Range (used): $8,000–$15,000 for WRX; higher for STI models.
  • Maintenance: Moderate; turbo engines require careful upkeep.

    • Mitsubishi Lancer Evo VIII

    produced between 2003 and 2005, is a high-performance car that attracts enthusiasts willing to invest more in tuning.

  • Engine Options: 2.0L Turbocharged Inline-4 (4G63T).
  • Price Range (used): $15,000–$30,000 depending on mileage and condition.
  • Maintenance: Moderate to high; performance parts are more expensive.
